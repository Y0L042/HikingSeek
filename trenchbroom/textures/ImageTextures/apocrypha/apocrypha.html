Construction
Base: New textures from scratch.
Usage
You can use these textures in any way you deem suitable. Cut, paste, combine and recolor to your heart's content.
Many of the textures appear as variants, sometimes including the seamless tessellation origin texture from which I created the more detailed images. These can form the basis for a map theme on their own, but there is no intention that you should restrict use of these textures to only what appears in the wad.
Number of textures: 170
